Christianity
What is Christianity?

Christianity is the world's biggest religion, with about 2.1 billion followers worldwide. It is based on the teachings of
Jesus Christ who lived in the Holy Land 2,000 years ago. Followers of this religion are called Christians. Christians
believe that Jesus is the Son of God who came as a messiah.
In Trinidad and Tobago, there are many different Christian denominations. The following are just some of the categories:
  1. Roman Catholic Church
  2. Anglican Church
  3. Presbyterian Church
  4. Methodist Church
  5. Evangelical Baptist
  6. The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day
  7. Association of Jehovah's Witnesses
