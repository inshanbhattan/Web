Culture: A Review of Our Religion
What is Culture?

Culture can be defined as the sum of attitudes, customs, and beliefs that distinguishes one group of people from another. This website will focus on exploring religion as one major aspect of culture

This website will focus on three major religions: Islam, Christianity and Hinduism.
